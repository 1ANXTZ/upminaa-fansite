Biography

Name Mina, better known as UpMinaa
From Germany 🇩🇪
Age 20 years old
Streaming since February 2023

UpMinaa's journey began in late 2022, when a single Gawr Gura cosplay video went viral on TikTok. What was meant to be just a post turned into the start of a multi-platform career: today she's amassed millions of followers combined across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Twitch, and X.

She officially launched her Twitch channel in February 2023 and, starting from zero, built a base of nearly 200,000 followers in a completely organic way — not by chasing numbers, but through genuine connection with her audience.

Fun Facts

  • She's bilingual — switching between German and English on stream depending on who's in chat.
  • She blends cosplay with horror, often playing horror games while fully in character.
  • A self-proclaimed anime fan, with Blue Lock, Tokyo Ghoul, Naruto, and Jujutsu Kaisen among her favorites.
  • Recurring references in her cosplays include Hatsune Miku and vtuber Gawr Gura.
  • She usually goes live on Wednesdays, Saturdays, and Sundays around 7 PM (CET) — though she admits to running late sometimes.
